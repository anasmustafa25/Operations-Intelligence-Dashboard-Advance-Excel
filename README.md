𝐎𝐯𝐞𝐫𝐯𝐢𝐞𝐰
This project simulates real-world enterprise data analysis. Starting with a massive, highly fragmented data ecosystem full of missing values, duplicates, and mixed formatting, the data was programmatically cleaned, engineered using advanced statistical formulas, and consolidated into a dynamic executive-level dashboard.

It tracks over 6 million in total sales and bridges operational performance with data-driven decision-making.

🔍 𝐅𝐞𝐚𝐭𝐮𝐫𝐞𝐬
🧼 Comprehensive Data Cleansing: Handled duplicates, multi-format dates, text standardizations, and error isolation across 6 nested data matrices.

📈 Executive KPIs: Real-time visibility into Total Sales, Total Orders, Average Delivery Time, Return Percentages, and Low Stock Alerts.

🎯 Performance Ranks: Automated evaluation logic grading employee performance and ranking regional supply chains.

🚚 Shipping & Modal Analytics: Granular breakdown of delivery durations, shipment costs, and delayed order bottlenecks.

⚡ Dynamic Filters & Slicers: Fully interactive slicers and timelines that dynamically update multi-layered cross-sheet data visualizations.

🛠️ 𝐓𝐨𝐨𝐥𝐬 & 𝐅𝐨𝐫𝐦𝐮𝐥𝐚𝐬 𝐔𝐬𝐞𝐝
1. Data Engineering & Analytics Architecture
Data Cleansing: TRIM, PROPER, UPPER, CLEAN, and conditional removal of duplicates/missing records.

Lookup & Reference: Complex multi-sheet mapping using XLOOKUP, INDEX, MATCH, and dynamic arrays (UNIQUE, FILTER, SORT).

Logical & Conditional Analysis: IFS, AND, OR, SUMIFS, COUNTIFS, and AVERAGEIFS for segmented performance tracking.

Statistical Modeling & Error Handling: RANK, PERCENTILE, LARGE, SMALL, IFERROR, and ISBLANK.

2. Visualization & Reporting Engine
Pivot Tables & Matrix Charts: Tailored visualizations including Column, Bar, Line, Doughnut, and advanced Combo charts.

Interactive UI/UX: Custom KPI cards, cross-filtering Slicers, interactive Timelines, Data Validation rules, and Conditional Formatting layers.

📁 𝐃𝐚𝐬𝐡𝐛𝐨𝐚𝐫𝐝 Architecture & Sheets
The core system processes operational data across 6 fundamental transactional streams:

🛒 Orders: Sales pipelines, revenue generation, and transaction history.

📦 Inventory: Warehouse stock levels, unit pricing, and depletion rates.

👥 Employees: Department allocations, salary structures, and experience tracking.

🏭 Suppliers: Procurement efficiency and manufacturer details.

🚛 Logistics: Shipping modes, freight costs, and duration trackers.

↩️ Returns: Post-purchase analysis and return reason frequencies.
